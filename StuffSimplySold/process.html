Phone/Email Conversation

I ask some brief questions about what you are looking to consign and schedule a walkthrough to look at the items in further depth

WalkThrough

15 minute meeting to look at the items you are looking at consigning and I provide my opinion on how much I think they could sell for

Selling Period

A set period of time we decide upon that I sell your items

End Of Selling Period

After the selling period is complete I will provide you with payment, a receipt of each item and how much it sold for, the option to keep the items that didn't sell or a contact to donate them and the organization will pick the items up
