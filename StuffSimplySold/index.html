Stuff Simply Sold is a premier consignment business based in Fort Myers, Florida, specializing in furniture consignment while also accepting clothing and various other items. Dedicated to simplifying the selling process, they offer a hassle-free platform for individuals to consign their unwanted items and receive fair compensation, making moving and decluttering easier for their clients.

About Me

My journey began with a simple problem: what to do with furniture during a move. Seeing the demand for quality second-hand goods, I founded Stuff Simply Sold to make selling your stuff a breeze. What makes my shop special? It's my dedication to making sure every customer walks away satisfied. From our first chat to the final sale, I'm here to make the process smooth and stress-free. In a nutshell, my journey from dealing with moving headaches to running Stuff Simply Sold is all about bringing innovation and empathy to the world of consignment. Through my shop, I aim to simplify buying and selling pre-loved items, one consignment at a time.

- Lisa Riley (Owner)
